Product Description

Our state-of-the-art Digital X-ray Machine provides exceptional image quality with significantly reduced radiation exposure. Designed for both small clinics and large hospitals, this system offers unparalleled diagnostic capabilities in a compact footprint.

Advanced Imaging Technology

The system features a high-resolution flat panel detector that captures detailed images with excellent contrast resolution. The advanced image processing algorithms enhance diagnostic information while maintaining natural tissue appearance.

Ergonomic Design

With its space-saving design and intuitive user interface, this X-ray system improves workflow efficiency. The motorized positioning allows for easy patient positioning with minimal effort.

Safety Features

The system includes multiple safety features including automatic exposure control, collimation indicators, and dose monitoring to ensure patient and operator safety.
